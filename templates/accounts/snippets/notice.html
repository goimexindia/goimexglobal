Buyers Tip
  1. Selection of appropriate key words at the time of buy offer posting, you can make it easier for suppliers to search your product requirements.
  2. Subscribe to your interested Product Alert and you will get emails with the latest and hottest products information periodically.
  3. Our buying request channel helps you trade easier. Suppliers who can provide items you need will reply the request and give quotations to you.

Suppliers Tip
  1. Display your products variety, and increase the opportunity to attain genuine inquiries from buyers.
  2. You need to be very selective and appropriate in case of choosing the product Title/Name. Select the combination of (generic + specific keywords) to make it easily searchable for buyers.
  3. Product optimization aids the product to be more attractive, searchable and easy to find by importers, buyers and traders.
